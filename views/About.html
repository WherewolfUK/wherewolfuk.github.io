Wherewolf UK is split across four groups in the South of England.

We all play games on a regular basis in our local cities, and meet up twice a year for larger events such as Wherewolfest in the summer, and Wherewolfcon in the winter.

Brighton

The Brighton group is one of the largest of the Wherewolf UK groups, meeting at the Caxton Arms every Monday night to play.

Find out more on the Brighton Meetup page

Bristol

The Bristol group is another of the larger Wherewolf UK groups, meeting at the Ship Inn on alternating Mondays and Sundays. Best to check the calendar to find out when they'll be there!

Find out more on the Bristol Meetup page

Oxford

The Oxford group meets once a fortnight on Mondays or Wednesdays at the Kite Inn. Make sure to check the calendar to find the next event!

Find out more on the Oxford Meetup page

London

The London group meets at St. Christopher's Inn. Make sure to check the calendar to find the next event!

Find out more on the London Meetup page
